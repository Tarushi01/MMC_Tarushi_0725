Login Component

Secure login systems are essential in online banking to protect user data and prevent fraud. Secure login systems are the backbone of online banking. They enable users to access their accounts through encrypted portals using credentials like usernames, passwords, and increasingly, biometric data. Modern banking platforms integrate multi-factor authentication and session tracking to prevent unauthorized access. The evolution of login technology—from simple password entry to fingerprint and facial recognition—reflects the industry's commitment to balancing convenience with robust cybersecurity
